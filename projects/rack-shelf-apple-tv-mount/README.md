# Rack Shelf Apple TV Mount

A 3D-printable mount for attaching an Apple TV to a 1U 19" rack shelf using existing ventilation slots.

## Features

- Snap-fit clips attach to standard rack shelf ventilation slots (35.5mm × 5mm)
- No drilling or permanent modifications to rack shelf
- Ventilation grid for passive cooling
- Cable management slot at rear
- Corner retaining clips keep Apple TV secure
- Optimized for 3D printing (minimal overhangs, 45° max angles)

## Specifications

### Shelf Compatibility
- Slot dimensions: 35.5mm × 5mm
- Slot spacing: 20mm center-to-center
- Shelf thickness: 1mm (SPCC steel)

### Apple TV 4K (3rd Gen)
- Width: 93.0mm
- Depth: 93.0mm
- Height: 31.0mm
- Weight: 208-214g

## Printing

**Recommended Settings:**
- Layer Height: 0.20mm
- Material: PLA or PETG
- Infill: 20%
- Supports: None required
- Print Time: ~3-4 hours

**Important:** The clips are designed with thin flex sections for snap-fit action. Print orientation is already optimized in the model.

## Assembly

1. Print the mount
2. Locate desired position on rack shelf ventilation slots
3. Align clips with slots
4. Press firmly to snap clips into place
5. Place Apple TV on mount - corner clips and side lips keep it secure
6. Route power/HDMI cables through rear cable management slot
